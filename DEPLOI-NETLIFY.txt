  for = "/content/site.json"
  [headers.values]
    Cache-Control = "no-store"

[[headers]]
  for = "/assets/*"
  [headers.values]

💡 TIPS

• Every time you change **content/site.json**, Netlify automatically updates your live site.  
• Use **Cmd + Shift + R** (Mac) or **Ctrl + F5** (Windows) to hard refresh.  
• You can connect your own custom domain later under **Domain settings**.
